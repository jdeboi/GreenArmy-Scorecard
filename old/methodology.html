Bill Selection

A GreenARMY committee picked out bills that were voted on by both the House and the Senate, and that pertained to the GreenARMY mission of clean air, clean water, healthy land and food. Many of the bills of interest in the 2014 legislative session pertained to the Southeast Louisiana Flood Protection Authority - East (SLFPAE) lawsuit, a lawsuit that the GreenARMY supported as a way to protect our diminishing coast and a lawsuit that Jindal and much of the legislature sought to dismantle.

Score Calculation

The score calculation is simple. It's a percentaged derived from the number of times the legislator voted with the GreenARMY divided by the number of times the legislator voted with and against the GA (absent votes are not included in the calculation of the score).
