The GreenARMY would like to thank all of the legislators who sponsored bills designed to defend Louisiana's clean water, clean air, clean land and food - the tenets upon which the GreenARMY stands and the foundation of our fight. The 2014 legislative session was rife with victories, and also learning experiences, but our supporters should know that the GreenARMY's work is just beginning. Louisiana's pollution problem demands a proglonged, steady, and robust grassroots movement, and we are fully committed to the fight.

Below you'll find the "GreenTEAM" - five state senators and five representatives with the highest scores - an indication of their consistent support of the GreenARMY, our legislation, and our movement. These leaders made our victories possible; we thank them for standing with the GreenARMY, and we look forward to working with them in 2015.
